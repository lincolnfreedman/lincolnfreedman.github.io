## About Me

I'm a specialist in technical production and management, especially for agile workflows and software-centric projects.

My programming language repertoire includes C#, C++, Python, Java, and Javascript. I'm well versed in version control dynamics and team organization.

I really love games! Especially strategy games like Starcraft 2, Library of Ruina, and Pokemon. I have a lot of experience developing in Unity and Unreal.

I graduated from Northeastern University with an MS in Game Science & Design, which taught me a great deal about coordinating between subteams (art, sound, engineering, UX), iterative production, testing, objective pivots, dynamic planning, and so forth.

I have a background in robot code and enjoy tackling complex technical problems at multiple levels. I am also a very creative person and a lover of art. My BA is in Film & Media Studies, so I'm well trained in communication, marketing, and presentation.
